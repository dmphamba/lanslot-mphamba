I'm Lanslot Mphamba — a seasoned business leader, consultant, and academic with over four decades of impact across banking, consultancy, and higher education.

My career began at Barclays Bank of Zimbabwe in 1984, where I rose from frontline banking to senior operations and internal audit leadership. In 2004, I transitioned into independent consulting, advising organisations ranging from SMEs to NGOs on financial strategy, governance, and operational transformation.

As a lecturer, I delivered MBA programs affiliated with Nottingham Trent University and guided over 40 MBA students through research and management application projects. I hold a PhD in Business Administration from UNICAF University and an MBA from Nottingham Trent University (UK).

Beyond the boardroom, I am an author of 6 Christian books, five of which are listed on Amazon — and a passionate advocate for economic and social transformation across communities and organisations.
